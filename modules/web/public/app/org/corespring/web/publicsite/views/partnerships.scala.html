CoreSpring is actively seeking partner relationships to fast-forward efforts to standardize
the way assessment content is delivered in classrooms.

Content Developers

CoreSpring plans to support the nation’s transition to college-ready standards and by hosting assessment content across core and non-core subjects. We’re accepting donations to the CoreSpring’s open collections and we're also interested in hearing from partners who may want to curate and distribute private collections using the CoreSpring platform and tools.

Early Adopters

CoreSpring wants to actively engage educators in the design and development process. We’ll grant access to the tools and content for use in your classroom in exchange for candid feedback on how to improve CoreSpring for you and your students.

Technologists

CoreSpring would like to collaborate with platform developers who see an assessment solution as critical to success. (Check out our developer page to learn more about our suite of APIs and about our Item Player.)

Administrators

Technology is increasingly playing a role in an Administrator’s strategy and CoreSpring can support these efforts. For example, we want to work with you to support your transition to the Common Core State Standards.
