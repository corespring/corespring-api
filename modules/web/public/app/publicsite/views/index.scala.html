Curated Collections. College-Ready Content. Innovative Design and Delivery.

Find Quality Content

Content from crowdsourced and unvetted banks often don't pay off. Educators can search the CoreSpring Collections, shared items sourced from trusted providers. The items are universally tagged so that educators can compare content using important criteria (e.g., aligned standards, Bloom’s Taxonomy, key skills, and demonstrated knowledge).

Administer with Ease

CoreSpring can be used in any browser-capable environment which means that bubble sheets, scanners, cameras, or other contraptions typically used to capture and score responses, are not needed. As soon as a student responds to an assessment question, the response is scored, stored, and ready for immediate review.

Get Actionable Data

After students submit responses, items are scored and educators can conduct preliminary item analysis to understand student outcomes more clearly. For example, educators can review aggregate information such as the “top 5 common incorrect answers” or individual student work, with just a click.
