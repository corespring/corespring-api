CoreSpring Collections

Some of Our Quality Content Providers:

At CoreSpring we're building a library of formative assessment items that help educators and students achieve deeper learning in classrooms. All CoreSpring interactions reflect the designs presented in PARCC and Smarter Balanced samples and can include a variety of media for maximum engagement. To start, CoreSpring is building a collection of items across K–12 in Mathematics, English Language Arts, Literacy, and Science. Items can be accompanied by supporting materials such as Scoring Guides, Rubrics, and examples of work.

Reliable Rendering and Automatic Scoring

CoreSpring items can be integrated in any browser-based platform. Items are ”played” in browser engines on PC/laptop/tablets devices (specific tablet/touchscreen device administration enhancements will be added to standard browser-based delivery). We do this so that items can be consistently rendered-what you see is what you get.

Item Authoring

Items are authored very quickly and easily using CoreSpring’s authoring tools. The system allows authors to select an interaction, design feedback, add scoring rules, and supporting materials.

Item Banking

All users of CoreSpring get access to the shared collections and organizations can store, manage, and share items with smaller networks in Private Collections.
