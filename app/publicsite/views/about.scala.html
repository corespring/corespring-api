Quality content is an essential input to effective data-driven instructional environments. CoreSpring organizes assessment items using a common template that aligns content against standards such as the Common Core State Standards and frameworks that identify the targeted cognitive ability such as Bloom's taxonomy, and presents community feedback so that community members can make informed choices.

Save Time

Create quizzes aligned with instructional objectives by searching for and finding content in CoreSpring's open collections of items and activities, or create items from scratch. Over time, CoreSpring community members will have access to a wide variety of content across a full range of grades and subjects.

Be Confident

The nation is focused on higher levels of performance required for success in college. Be sure that the CoreSpring collections are designed to helps teachers and students achieve these goals.

Focus on the Future

CoreSpring content can be delivered using CoreSpring’s developer tools. CoreSpring is forward thinking, aligned with important standards such as QTI and LTI, and engaged with the Learning Resource Metadata Initiative (LRMI) and the Strategic Learning Collaborative (SLC).

Eliminate the Headaches

When using CoreSpring, there’s no need to search through multiple sites and databases or deal with different formats to find good material. CoreSpring finds the good material, secures, the permissions, and digitizes and tags the content so that you can focus on the more important things.
