Innovative Design and Delivery.

 

Save Time

Make quizzes aligned with instructional objectives by searching for and finding content in CoreSpring's open collection of items and activities or, create items from scratch. Over time, CoreSpring community members will have access to a range of content across a full range of grades and subjects.

Focus on the Future

With the nation's focus on higher levels of performance required for success in college, CoreSpring helps to prepare students for the future by delivering a variety of item types -including performance items. The content can be delivered online through other data platforms using our APIs and/or via the CoreSpring website. The technology collects usage data and aggregates it to help develop a picture of what is or isn't working for students.

Be Confident

Quality content is an essential input to effective data-driven instructional environments. To ensure quality, CoreSpring organizes assessment items using a common template. The template aligns items and activities against standards such as the Common Core State Standards, frameworks that identify the targeted cognitive ability such as Bloom's taxonomy, and presents community feedback so that community members can make informed choices.

Eliminate the Headaches

We're working with leaders in the education field to develop a comprehensive collection located in one place. You won't need to search through multiple sites and databases or deal with different formats to find the good stuff. In addition, CoreSpring's test player presents a predictable and consistent look and feel-what you see is what you get.
