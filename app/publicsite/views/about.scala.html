Here are some answers to some frequently asked questions.

How does CoreSpring manage for quality?

CoreSpring makes every attempt to include items from regarded authors. After items are transformed into a digital format, content experts review contributions and align them against a common rubric.

What is CoreSpring technology?

CoreSpring's Item Player can be integrated with any web-based system. This ensures that items look, feel and behave consistently on all systems, particularly items that use more advanced interaction types. Our REST API provides developer flexibility in integrating CoreSpring content seamlessly. Please visit our developers page for more information.

Is CoreSpring a nonprofit initiative?

CoreSpring will seek tax-exempt status and we will build toward sustainability so that we may continuously improve the bank and the offering. In the future we anticipate that there will be some fees associated with using CoreSpring.

Does CoreSpring cover all subjects and all grades Pre-K-12+?

To build the best bank, CoreSpring is starting with Math and English-Language Arts Literacy in grades K-12. We are actively seeking partnerships that expand our library. Please visit our partnerships page for more information.

Where does CoreSpring find content?

CoreSpring is aggregating content from a number of places like state agencies and educational service organizations. When sourcing content, we prioritize quality and rigor and select materials designed specifically for college-ready standards like the Common Core State Standards.

Does CoreSpring support a variety of item types?

At CoreSpring, multiple-choice is not a bad word but we know that students need exposure to a variety of tasks. Our Item Player supports the majority of interactions included in the PARCC and Smarter Balanced samples and we are constantly updating our own library so that content authors may create interactive and engaging experiences for students. Please visit our educators page to browse a few samples.

Is CoreSpring an assessment platform?

Think of CoreSpring as one component of a larger teaching and learning solution. CoreSpring is building a collection of items that can be used as a source for great content within the context of other platforms like Learning Management Systems.

Does CoreSpring access student data?

CoreSpring does not have access to or manage any identifiable data about students.
